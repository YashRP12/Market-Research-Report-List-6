<p><strong>The Masking Protective Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Masking Protective Film</li><li>Liquid Masking Protective Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Masking Protective Film market consists of two primary types: solid and liquid masking films. Solid masking protective films are typically made from materials like polyethylene or vinyl, offering physical barriers to protect surfaces during processes such as painting or coating. In contrast, liquid masking protective films are applied as a spray or liquid solution that dries to form a protective layer, easily conforming to irregular surfaces. Both types serve to shield surfaces from damage, contamination, and overspray in various industrial applications.</p></p>
