<p><p>The Automotive HVAC Module market can be categorized into two main types: Direct Blowing Type and Duct Type. The Direct Blowing Type delivers conditioned air directly to the cabin, providing quick temperature regulation, often used in smaller vehicles. In contrast, the Duct Type utilizes a network of ducts to distribute air throughout the cabin, offering a more uniform temperature control, typically favored in larger vehicles. Both types are essential for enhancing passenger comfort and vehicle efficiency.</p></p>
