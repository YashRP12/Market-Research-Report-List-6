<p><p>Copper ion selective electrodes (Cu ISEs) are used for measuring copper concentrations in various applications, such as environmental monitoring and industrial processes. The market is primarily categorized into two types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize organic solvents to selectively transport copper ions, offering high sensitivity and rapid response. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes incorporate polyvinyl chloride as the matrix, providing stability and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for routine analysis in laboratories and field testing.</p></p>
