CVT-based systems offer seamless acceleration and fuel efficiency, making them ideal for hybrid vehicles that prioritize smooth driving experiences. In contrast, DCT-based systems provide quicker gear shifts and enhanced performance, suited for sportier hybrid options. Both technologies aim to optimize energy efficiency and enhance driving dynamics, catering to diverse consumer preferences in the hybrid vehicle segment.</p></p>
