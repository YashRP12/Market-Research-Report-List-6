<p><p>The aircraft altimeter market is divided into two primary types: analog and digital altimeters. Analog altimeters utilize mechanical components to display altitude through a dial and needle, providing a straightforward, reliable readout. In contrast, digital altimeters use electronic sensors and displays, offering enhanced precision, data integration, and ease of use. The digital type is increasingly favored in modern aviation for its advanced features, while analog devices remain popular in certain applications for their simplicity and robustness.</p></p>
