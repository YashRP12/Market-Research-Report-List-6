<p><p>The Optical Film for LCD Backlight Module market includes three main types: reflective film, diffusion membrane, and light guide membrane. Reflective film enhances brightness by reflecting light back into the display, improving energy efficiency. Diffusion membranes scatter light evenly across the surface, ensuring uniform brightness and color consistency. Light guide membranes facilitate the even distribution of light from LEDs, optimizing backlight performance and enhancing image quality. Together, these films play a crucial role in the functionality and visual appeal of LCD displays.</p></p>
