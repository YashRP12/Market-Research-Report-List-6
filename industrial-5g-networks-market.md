<p><p>Industrial 5G Networks have two main market types based on frequency bands: Sub-6 GHz and mmWave. Sub-6 GHz offers wider coverage and better penetration through obstacles, making it suitable for outdoor and indoor applications in industrial settings. On the other hand, mmWave operates at higher frequencies, offering faster speeds but with limited coverage and obstacle penetration. mmWave is best suited for high-density areas with high bandwidth requirements, such as factories or warehouses requiring ultra-fast data transmission.</p></p>
