<p><p>Non-invasive prenatal testing (NIPT) equipment refers to advanced diagnostic tools that analyze cell-free fetal DNA present in maternal blood to assess the risk of genetic conditions in the fetus, such as Down syndrome and other chromosomal abnormalities. This technology offers a safer alternative to traditional invasive methods like amniocentesis, significantly reducing the risk of miscarriage.</p><p>The Non-invasive Prenatal Testing Equipment Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.60% during the forecast period. Additionally, the availability of cost-effective NIPT solutions is expanding access to these tests in both developed and emerging markets.</p><p>Latest trends in the market include the introduction of next-generation sequencing technologies, which improve the accuracy and efficiency of tests.
