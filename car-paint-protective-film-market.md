<p><p>The car paint protective film market primarily features two types: Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) and Polyethylene (PE). In contrast, PE films offer a more cost-effective solution with decent protection, but they may not be as resilient as TPU. Both types serve to shield vehicles from environmental damage, enhancing longevity and maintaining aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
