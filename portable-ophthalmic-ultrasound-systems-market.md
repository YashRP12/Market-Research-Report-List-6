<p><p>Portable Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems are used for diagnostic imaging of the eye. There are different types of ultrasound scans available in the market including A-Scan, B-Scan, Combined Scan, Pachymeter, and Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM). </p><p>A-Scan is used to measure the length of the eyeball and diagnose conditions like cataracts. B-Scan produces cross-sectional images to detect abnormalities in the eye. Combined Scan combines A-Scan and B-Scan capabilities. Pachymeter measures corneal thickness and UBM provides detailed imaging of the anterior segment of the eye.</p></p>
