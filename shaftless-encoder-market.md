<p><p>Shaftless encoders are used to detect the position, speed, and direction of rotation of a motor or shaft without the need for a physical shaft connection. There are three main types of shaftless encoders: magnetic encoders, capacitive encoders, and optical encoders. </p><p>Magnetic encoders use magnets and sensors to detect the position of a rotating object. Capacitive encoders use changes in capacitance to determine position. Optical encoders use light and sensors to detect movement. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications.</p></p>
