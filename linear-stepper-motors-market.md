<p><p>Linear stepper motors are commonly categorized into two types based on their axis configurations: Single Axis Linear Stepper Motors and Dual Axis Linear Stepper Motors. </p><p>Single Axis Linear Stepper Motors are designed to move in a straight line along a single axis, making them ideal for applications where motion is required in one direction. </p><p>On the other hand, Dual Axis Linear Stepper Motors are capable of movement in two perpendicular axes, offering increased flexibility and precision in applications where multidirectional motion is necessary.</p></p>
