<p><p>The polypropylene woven bags market comprises two main types: laminated and non-laminated bags. Laminated polypropylene woven bags feature a protective coating, enhancing moisture resistance and durability, making them ideal for storing food or agricultural products. Non-laminated polypropylene woven bags, while less resistant to moisture, are more cost-effective and widely used for bulk materials like fertilizers, grains, and construction materials. Both types offer strength and versatility, catering to diverse industries and varying consumer needs.</p></p>
