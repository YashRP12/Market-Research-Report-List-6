<p><p>Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) sensors are analytical devices used to detect and measure specific gases based on their infrared absorption characteristics. These sensors operate by passing infrared light through a gas sample and measuring the amount of light absorbed at specific wavelengths corresponding to the target gas. NDIR sensors are widely utilized in environmental monitoring, industrial applications, and in areas like automotive emissions testing due to their efficacy, accuracy, and stability.</p><p>The NDIR sensor market is poised for significant growth, driven by increasing demand for environmental monitoring, stringent regulations on emissions, and the rising need for gas detection in various industries.
