<p><strong>The OLED Encapsulation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TFE</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED encapsulation system market is primarily divided into two types: TFE (Transparent Film Encapsulation) and hybrid encapsulation. TFE involves using transparent films that provide moisture and oxygen barrier protection while maintaining optical clarity, essential for OLED performance. In contrast, hybrid encapsulation combines both organic and inorganic materials, enhancing durability and protection against environmental factors. These technologies aim to enhance the longevity and efficiency of OLED displays by minimizing degradation caused by exposure to air and humidity.</p></p>
