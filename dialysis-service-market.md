<p><p>The Dialysis Service Market consists of two primary types: Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis. Hemodialysis involves using a machine to filter waste and excess fluid from the blood, typically conducted at a dialysis center or at home. Peritoneal Dialysis uses the lining of the abdominal cavity to filter blood, allowing patients to perform the treatment at home. Both methods cater to patients with kidney failure, providing options based on patient preference, lifestyle, and medical considerations.</p></p>
