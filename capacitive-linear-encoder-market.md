<p><p>The Capacitive Linear Encoder market comprises two main types: Axle Type and Shaft Type. Axle Type encoders are designed to measure linear position along axles, commonly used in automotive and industrial applications where accurate position tracking is essential. In contrast, Shaft Type encoders focus on rotating shafts, providing precise measurements of angular position and rotation. Both types leverage capacitive sensing technology to deliver high-resolution and reliable performance, making them vital in automation, robotics, and various precision measurement applications.</p></p>
