A 1-axis magnetic sensor detects magnetic fields along a single direction, making it ideal for basic applications. The 2-axis sensor measures fields in two directions, enhancing orientation and position sensing. The 3-axis magnetic sensor provides comprehensive magnetic field data in three dimensions, enabling advanced applications in robotics, automotive, and consumer electronics, where precise magnetic measurement is crucial.</p></p>
