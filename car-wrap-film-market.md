<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Car Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car wrap film market is primarily divided into two types: cast films and calendered films. Cast films are produced by pouring molten PVC onto a flat surface, resulting in a thinner, more flexible, and higher-quality product that offers superior conformability and durability. Calendered films, on the other hand, are made by passing PVC through rollers, resulting in a thicker and less flexible material, typically used for larger applications and offering cost advantages. Each type caters to different needs in vehicle customization.</p></p>
