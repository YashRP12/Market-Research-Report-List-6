SWIR area cameras capture two-dimensional images, making them ideal for applications like industrial inspection and surveillance. Both camera types leverage InGaAs technology for enhanced sensitivity in the SWIR spectrum, catering to diverse needs across industries like agriculture, defense, and telecommunications.</p></p>
