<p><p>Boilers, turbines, and generators are critical components in power generation. Power boilers produce steam by heating water, using fuels like coal or natural gas. Gas turbines convert gas fuel into mechanical energy, while steam turbines generate electricity by utilizing steam pressure. Turbo generators are integrated systems that combine turbines and generators for efficient power production. Heat recovery steam generators capture waste heat from processes, enhancing overall efficiency and sustainability in energy production. Each type plays a vital role in optimizing power generation systems.</p></p>
