<p><p>Radiation detection, monitoring, and safety market includes various types of devices such as Geiger Counters, Scintillation Detectors, Solid State Detectors, and others. Geiger Counters are widely used for detecting ionizing radiation. Scintillation Detectors use crystals to detect radiation by emitting light. Solid State Detectors are semiconductor-based detectors used for precise measurement of radiation levels. Other types of detectors include photoionization detectors, thermal neutron detectors, and neutron activation detectors. These devices are essential for ensuring workplace safety and environmental monitoring in industries such as nuclear power, healthcare, and defense.</p></p>
