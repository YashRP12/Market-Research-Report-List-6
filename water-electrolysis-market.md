<p><strong>The Water Electrolysis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water electrolysis market comprises mainly two types: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers utilize a liquid alkaline electrolyte and are known for their durability and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for large-scale hydrogen production. In contrast, PEM electrolyzers employ a solid polymer electrolyte, allowing for higher efficiency and faster response times, making them ideal for renewable energy integration and smaller-scale applications. Both technologies play crucial roles in the growing hydrogen economy.</p></p>
