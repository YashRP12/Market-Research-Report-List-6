<p><p>Dry dust collectors are essential for controlling airborne particulate matter in industrial settings. Inertial separators operate by using centrifugal forces to separate dust from airflow. Electrostatic precipitators employ electrical fields to attract and collect charged particles. Fabric filters use a membrane or cloth to capture dust particles as air passes through. Other types may include cyclones, venturi scrubbers, and hybrid systems, each tailored to specific applications based on efficiency, cost, and dust characteristics.</p></p>
