<p><p>The brown rice market is categorized into three main types based on grain length: short, medium, and long grain. Short grain brown rice is plump and sticky when cooked, ideal for sushi and risottos. Medium grain brown rice has a slightly chewy texture and is versatile for various dishes. Long grain brown rice remains fluffy and separate once cooked, commonly used in pilafs and stir-fries. Additionally, there are other specialty varieties, including organic and heirloom types, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
