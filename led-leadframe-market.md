<p><p>LED Leadframe market consists of two main types: Simplex Metal Lead Frame and Resin Molding Lead Frame. Simplex Metal Lead Frame is made of a single metal sheet that provides high thermal conductivity and mechanical strength. On the other hand, Resin Molding Lead Frame is made of thermosetting resin materials that offer high flexibility in design and manufacturing. Both types cater to different needs in the LED industry, with metal lead frames being preferred for high-power applications and resin lead frames for cost-effective solutions.</p></p>
