<p><p>The pistachio nuts market is primarily divided into two types based on fruit size: short fruit and long fruit pistachios. Short fruit pistachios are characterized by smaller, rounder kernels, offering a sweeter taste and often used in gourmet products. In contrast, long fruit pistachios have elongated kernels, providing a more substantial bite, and are commonly favored in bulk consumption and snack products. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, influencing their market demand and pricing strategies.</p></p>
