<p><p>The casting current transformer market comprises two main types: measuring current transformers and protective current transformers. Measuring current transformers are designed for accurate current measurement in electrical systems, enabling monitoring, billing, and performance analysis. In contrast, protective current transformers are used to detect faults and protect equipment by triggering circuit breakers or alarms. Both types are vital for ensuring the reliability and safety of electrical infrastructure, serving distinct but complementary roles in power management and protection.</p></p>
