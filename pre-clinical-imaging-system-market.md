<p><p>The preclinical imaging system market comprises two main types: standalone imaging systems and multimodal imaging systems. This enhances diagnostic accuracy and research capabilities by allowing for a more holistic view of the subject, facilitating the study of complex biological processes in preclinical research.</p></p>
