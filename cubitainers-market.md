<p><p>Cubitainers are versatile containers made from either low density polyethylene (LDPE) or high density polyethylene (HDPE), each catering to different market needs. LDPE is flexible, offers excellent chemical resistance, and is commonly used for storing liquids and granules, making it ideal for the agricultural and food industries. HDPE, on the other hand, is sturdier, provides superior impact resistance, and is often preferred in packaging household chemicals and industrial products. Together, these materials meet diverse packaging demand across various sectors.</p></p>
