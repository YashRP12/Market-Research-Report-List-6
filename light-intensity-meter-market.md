<p><p>The light intensity meter market includes various types of sensors, prominently featuring normal photodiodes and silicon photodiodes. Normal photodiodes are used for general light measurement, offering broad sensitivity across visible wavelengths, making them suitable for various applications. Silicon photodiodes, on the other hand, are specifically designed for higher sensitivity in the visible spectrum, providing enhanced accuracy in light intensity readings. Both types are critical in sectors such as agriculture, photography, and environmental monitoring, where precise light measurement is essential.</p></p>
