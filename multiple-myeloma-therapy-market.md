<p><p>The Multiple Myeloma Therapy market comprises several treatment modalities. Targeted therapy focuses on specific genetic mutations within cancer cells, enhancing effectiveness while reducing harm to healthy cells. Chemotherapy uses drugs to kill rapidly dividing cells, including cancerous ones, though it may affect normal cells too. Radiotherapy employs high-energy radiation to target and destroy myeloma cells. Bone marrow transplant involves replacing unhealthy bone marrow with healthy cells to restore normal blood cell production. Other therapies may include immunotherapy or novel agents in development.</p></p>
