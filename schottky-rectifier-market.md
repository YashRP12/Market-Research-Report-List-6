<p><p>The Schottky rectifier market is typically categorized into two types: single and dual configurations. Single Schottky rectifiers consist of a single diode, providing efficient rectification for low-voltage applications with fast switching capabilities. In contrast, dual Schottky rectifiers integrate two diodes in a single package, allowing for compact designs and enabling applications that require higher current handling and reduced thermal resistance. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics, emphasizing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
