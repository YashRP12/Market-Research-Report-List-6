<p><p>Slack wax is a semi-solid wax derived from the petroleum refining process, categorized into three market types: Slack Wax LMO (Light Mineral Oil), Slack Wax MMO (Medium Mineral Oil), and Slack Wax SPO (Solid Paraffin Oil). Slack Wax LMO contains lower viscosity, suitable for applications like candles and coatings. Slack Wax MMO has a moderate oil content, ideal for emulsions and industrial lubricants. Slack Wax SPO is more solid, used primarily in packaging and cosmetic formulations due to its rigidity and stability.</p></p>
