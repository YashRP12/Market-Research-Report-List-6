<p><p>X-ray fluorescence (XRF) coating thickness gauges are essential tools for measuring the thickness of coatings on various substrates. The common type utilizes direct X-ray excitation to assess coating thickness, making it suitable for a wide range of materials and applications. The polycapillary type, on the other hand, employs polycapillary optics to focus and direct X-rays, enhancing sensitivity and precision, particularly for thin coatings or small areas. Both types play crucial roles in quality control across industries like manufacturing and aerospace.</p></p>
