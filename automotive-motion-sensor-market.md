<p><strong>The Automotive Motion Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Accelerometer</li><li>Gyroscope</li><li>Combo Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive motion sensor market includes three main types: accelerometers, gyroscopes, and combo sensors. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration, helping to detect vehicle movements and changes in speed. Gyroscopes measure rotational motion, aiding in stability control and navigation systems. Combo sensors integrate both accelerometer and gyroscopic functionalities, providing comprehensive data for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and enhancing safety features. Together, these sensors play a critical role in improving vehicle performance, safety, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
