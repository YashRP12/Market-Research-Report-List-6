Wind power hubs are central to turbine function, facilitating blade rotation. Pedestals provide structural support and stability for turbines. Gearbox castings are critical for transmitting mechanical energy from the blades to the generator, enhancing efficiency. Additionally, other castings may include various components essential for turbine operation and maintenance, contributing to the overall reliability and performance of wind energy systems.</p></p>
