<p><p>The polypropylene filtration fabric market is primarily segmented into two types: polypropylene monofilament filtration fabric and polypropylene multifilament filtration fabric. Monofilament filtration fabric is characterized by single, continuous strands, providing high strength and excellent filtration efficiency, often used in demanding applications. In contrast, multifilament filtration fabric consists of multiple intertwined fibers, offering greater flexibility and surface area, making it suitable for a variety of industrial and commercial filtration needs. Both types are widely used for liquid and air filtration processes.</p></p>
