<p><p>The metal detector market for packets primarily includes three technology types: electromagnetic induction, X-ray, and microwave detection. Electromagnetic induction detects metal through changes in magnetic fields, making it effective for various materials. X-ray detection, on the other hand, provides detailed imaging of package contents, identifying both metals and non-metals. Microwave detection utilizes electromagnetic waves to identify materials based on their unique signatures, allowing for fast and accurate differentiation of metal types and other substances. Each technology serves distinct applications in security and quality control.</p></p>
