<p><p>The aircraft brake discs market primarily comprises two types: carbon-ceramic and carbon-carbon. Carbon-ceramic brake discs are known for their lightweight properties and exceptional thermal stability, making them ideal for high-performance aircraft. They offer excellent wear resistance and longevity, reducing maintenance costs. On the other hand, carbon-carbon brake discs, made entirely from carbon fibers, provide superior heat resistance and performance under extreme conditions but are heavier and typically more expensive. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient and reliable aircraft braking systems.</p></p>
