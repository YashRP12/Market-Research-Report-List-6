<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Voltage Relay</li><li>Static Voltage Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low Voltage Relay market consists of two main types: Electromagnetic Voltage Relay and Static Voltage Relay. Electromagnetic Voltage Relay works by using an electromagnetic coil to open or close circuits based on voltage fluctuations. On the other hand, Static Voltage Relay operates without any moving parts, using semiconductor devices to detect and control voltage levels. Both types of relays are commonly used in control systems to protect electrical equipment from overloads and faults, ensuring smooth and reliable operation.</p></p>
