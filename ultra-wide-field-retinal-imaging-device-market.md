<p><strong>The Ultra Wide Field Retinal Imaging Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OCT Camera</li><li>Fundus Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Wide Field Retinal Imaging Device market primarily includes two key types: OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) cameras and fundus cameras. OCT cameras utilize light waves to capture detailed cross-sectional images of the retina, enabling precise diagnosis of various eye conditions. Fundus cameras, on the other hand, capture wide-field images of the retina's surface, allowing for comprehensive examination of retinal disorders. Both technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities, providing ophthalmologists with critical insights for effective patient treatment and management.</p></p>
