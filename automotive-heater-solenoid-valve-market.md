<p><ul><li>Direct Acting Type</li><li>Internally Piloted Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive heater solenoid valve market features two main types: direct acting and internally piloted. The direct acting type operates directly from the solenoid's electromagnetic force, allowing for quick response and precise control of coolant flow. In contrast, the internally piloted type uses a pilot pressure system, which provides enhanced reliability and reduced power consumption. Both types are crucial for regulating cabin temperature and improving passenger comfort in vehicles, catering to varying automotive design requirements and consumer preferences.</p></p>
