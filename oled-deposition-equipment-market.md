<p><p>The OLED deposition equipment market comprises several technologies, including Organic Vapor Phase Deposition (OVPD), Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD), and Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD). OVPD utilizes vaporized organic materials to create thin films, enabling precise layer control. PVD involves physical change processes, such as evaporation or sputtering, to deposit materials onto substrates. PECVD combines plasma energy with chemical vapor deposition to enhance film quality and uniformity. Other methods may include laser deposition and printing technologies, catering to diverse applications in OLED manufacturing.</p></p>
