<p><strong>The Machine Tool Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Probe</li><li>Non-Contact Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machine tool probe market is primarily divided into two types: contact probes and non-contact probes. Contact probes engage physically with the workpiece, providing precise measurements through direct touch. They are widely used in traditional machining environments for accurate dimensional checks. In contrast, non-contact probes utilize technologies like laser or optical sensors to measure without direct contact, offering advantages in speed and minimizing wear on both the probe and the workpiece. Both types enhance manufacturing precision and efficiency.</p></p>
