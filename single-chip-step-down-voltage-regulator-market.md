<p><p>The single-chip step-down voltage regulator market is divided into two primary types: AC and DC step-down voltage regulators. AC step-down voltage regulators convert higher alternating current (AC) voltage to lower AC voltage, commonly used in household devices and lighting applications. In contrast, DC step-down voltage regulators reduce higher direct current (DC) voltage to a lower level, essential for powering electronic devices like smartphones and microcontrollers. Both types focus on efficiency and compact design to meet the growing demand for power management solutions.</p></p>
