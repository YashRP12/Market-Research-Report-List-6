<p><p>An All-Flash Array (AFA) is a storage system that uses only flash memory to store data, providing high speed, performance, and efficiency for data-intensive applications. Unlike traditional storage systems that may incorporate hard disk drives, AFAs leverage the advantages of solid-state drives (SSDs) to deliver lower latency, faster data access, and reduced power consumption. This makes them ideal for environments with demanding workloads, such as virtualization, big data analytics, and high-frequency trading.</p><p>The All-Flash Array Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period. As companies prioritize digital transformation, AFAs are becoming a cornerstone of modern data infrastructure.</p></p>
