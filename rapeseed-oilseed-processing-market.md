<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Chemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rapeseed oilseed processing involves two primary market types: mechanical and chemical. Mechanical processing utilizes physical methods, such as cold pressing, to extract oil while retaining more nutrients, often producing high-quality oils favored in artisanal and premium markets. In contrast, chemical processing employs solvents, typically hexane, to extract oil more efficiently and at a larger scale, resulting in refined oils suitable for industrial and commercial applications. Each method serves distinct market demands based on quality, cost, and production volume.</p></p>
