<p><ul><li>Glass Bead Type</li><li>Micro Prismatic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retroreflective tapes market primarily consists of two types: glass bead and micro prismatic types. Glass bead tapes utilize small glass spheres embedded in a resin layer to reflect light, providing moderate visibility and durability. Micro prismatic tapes, on the other hand, employ advanced prism technology, offering superior brightness and visibility through effective light reflection. These tapes are used in various applications, including traffic signage, safety wear, and vehicle markings, enhancing safety and visibility in low-light conditions.</p></p>
