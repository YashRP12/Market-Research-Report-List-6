<p><p>The ASIC (Application-Specific Integrated Circuit) chip market consists of two main types: semi-custom and full customization. Semi-custom ASICs allow for a degree of pre-designed components, enabling faster production and reduced costs while maintaining a level of specificity for particular applications. In contrast, full-custom ASICs are tailor-made from the ground up, offering maximum design flexibility and performance optimization for niche requirements, albeit at higher development costs and longer lead times. Both cater to specialized electronic needs.</p></p>
