<p><strong>The Amorphous Core Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Immersed Amorphous Core Transformers</li><li>Dry-Type Amorphous Core Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amorphous core transformers utilize a unique core material that enhances efficiency and reduces energy loss. The market is segmented into two main types: oil-immersed and dry-type. Oil-immersed amorphous core transformers are submerged in insulating oil, providing enhanced cooling and protection against environmental factors, making them suitable for high voltage applications. Dry-type amorphous core transformers, on the other hand, use air as the cooling medium, offering improved safety and lower maintenance, making them ideal for indoor installations and urban areas.</p></p>
