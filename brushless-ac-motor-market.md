<p><strong>The Brushless AC Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Brushless AC Motors</li><li>Outer Rotor Brushless AC Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Brushless AC Motor market is categorized primarily into two types: Inner Rotor and Outer Rotor Brushless AC Motors. Inner Rotor Brushless AC Motors have the rotor positioned inside the stator, providing a compact design with higher torque density, making them suitable for applications like robotics and electric vehicles. In contrast, Outer Rotor Brushless AC Motors feature the rotor on the exterior, allowing for larger diameters and improved cooling, ideal for applications in fans, pumps, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
