<p><p>The metal carbide nanoparticles market includes various types, each with unique properties and applications. Zirconium carbide (ZrC) features high melting points and is used in high-temperature applications. Vanadium carbide (VC) offers wear resistance and strength, ideal for mining tools. Tantalum carbide (TaC) is valued for its thermal resistance in aerospace, while tungsten carbide (WC) is widely used in industrial applications due to its exceptional hardness.</p></p>
