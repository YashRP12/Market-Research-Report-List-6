<p><p>The high-speed baggage handling system market includes two primary types: RFID baggage transport and sortation systems and barcode baggage transport and sortation systems. RFID systems utilize radio-frequency identification technology to track and manage baggage in real-time, ensuring faster sorting and minimized loss. In contrast, barcode systems use printed labels scanned at various points to track luggage. Both systems enhance operational efficiency and accuracy in airports, reducing turnaround times and improving passenger experience during air travel.</p></p>
