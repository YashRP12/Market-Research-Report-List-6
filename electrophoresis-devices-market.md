<p><p>The electrophoresis devices market comprises primarily two types: gel electrophoresis systems and capillary electrophoresis systems. Gel electrophoresis systems utilize gel matrices to separate biomolecules, such as DNA, RNA, and proteins, based on size and charge, making them ideal for various applications in molecular biology. In contrast, capillary electrophoresis systems employ narrow capillaries to achieve rapid and high-resolution separation of analytes, allowing for efficient analysis in pharmaceuticals and clinical diagnostics. Both types enhance research and diagnostic capabilities in life sciences.</p></p>
