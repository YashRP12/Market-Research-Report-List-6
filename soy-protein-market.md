<p><p>The soy protein market comprises three main types: concentrated soy protein, isolated soy protein, and textured soy protein. Concentrated soy protein retains some carbohydrates and is commonly used in protein bars and meat alternatives. Isolated soy protein, purer with minimal carbohydrates and fats, is often utilized in protein powders and dietary supplements. Textured soy protein mimics meat texture, making it a popular choice for meat replacements in vegetarian and vegan products, catering to the growing demand for plant-based protein.</p></p>
