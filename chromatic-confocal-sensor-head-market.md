<p><strong>The Chromatic Confocal Sensor Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Displacement Sensor Head</li><li>Thickness Sensor Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chromatic confocal sensor head market includes two main types: displacement sensor heads and thickness sensor heads. Displacement sensor heads measure the distance between the sensor and an object, providing high precision in surface profiling and dimensional measurements. In contrast, thickness sensor heads are designed to measure the thickness of transparent or semi-transparent materials by analyzing light reflections at multiple wavelengths. Both types leverage chromatic confocal technology to enable non-contact, high-resolution measurement applications in various industries.</p></p>
