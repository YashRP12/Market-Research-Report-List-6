<p><p>The Subsea Blowout Preventer (BOP) market encompasses critical safety devices used in offshore drilling to prevent uncontrolled fluid release. The market is primarily divided into two types: Annular Blowout Preventers and Ram Blowout Preventers. Annular BOPs utilize a rubber sealing element to seal around the drill pipe, providing flexibility for various pipe sizes, while Ram BOPs use opposing steel plates, or "rams," to seal off the wellbore completely. Both types are essential for ensuring operational safety in subsea exploration.</p></p>
