<p><strong>The Digital X-Ray Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital X-Ray Systems market is primarily divided into two types: CR (Computed Radiography) tech and DR (Digital Radiography) tech systems. CR tech utilizes photographic plates that are scanned to produce digital images, offering a cost-effective solution for existing X-ray machines. In contrast, DR tech employs flat-panel detectors to capture images directly, providing superior image quality, faster processing times, and enhanced workflow efficiency. Both systems play integral roles in modern medical imaging, catering to varying healthcare facility needs.</p></p>
