<p><p>The financial auditing service market is primarily divided into two main types: external and internal audits. External audits are conducted by independent firms to provide an objective evaluation of a company's financial statements, ensuring accuracy and compliance with regulations, which is vital for stakeholders and investors. Internal audits, on the other hand, are performed by an organization’s own personnel to assess operational effectiveness and risk management, promoting internal controls and efficiency within the organization.
