<p><p>The photoelectric smoke detector market is primarily divided into two types: wireless smoke detectors and wired smoke detectors. Wireless smoke detectors utilize radio frequency technology for communication, allowing for easier installation and flexibility in placement without the need for extensive wiring. In contrast, wired smoke detectors are hardwired into the electrical system of a building, ensuring a constant power supply and often enhanced reliability. Both types serve to detect smoke through the photoelectric effect, enhancing safety in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
