<p><ul><li>Fuel Filter</li><li>Engine Air Filter</li><li>Oil Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive filter market includes three primary types: fuel filters, engine air filters, and oil filters. Fuel filters remove impurities from gasoline or diesel, ensuring clean fuel delivery to the engine. Engine air filters trap dust and debris, promoting optimal airflow for combustion and enhancing engine performance. Oil filters eliminate contaminants from engine oil, maintaining lubrication efficiency and prolonging engine life. Together, these filters are essential for vehicle functionality, performance, and longevity, contributing to overall automotive maintenance.</p></p>
