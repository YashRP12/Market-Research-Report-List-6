<p><p>The ambulatory cardiac monitor market includes several types. Holter monitors provide continuous 24-48 hour ECG recording for detecting arrhythmias. Event monitors are user-activated devices worn for longer periods, capturing ECG data during symptomatic episodes. Mobile cardiac telemetry offers real-time monitoring and immediate data transmission to healthcare providers. Other market types may include implantable loop recorders and smart wearable devices, which provide additional diagnostic capabilities and convenience. Each type serves distinct patient needs and enhances cardiac health monitoring.</p></p>
