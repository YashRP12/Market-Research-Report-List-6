<p><strong>The Particle Board Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solventless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The particle board adhesives market is categorized into three main types: solvent-based, water-based, and solventless adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding but raising environmental and health concerns. Water-based adhesives use water as a primary carrier, offering a safer and more eco-friendly option, though they may have lower performance in some applications. Solventless adhesives eliminate solvents entirely, resulting in minimal volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and making them increasingly popular for sustainable manufacturing practices.</p></p>
