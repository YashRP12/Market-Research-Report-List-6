<p><p>The Open Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) System market consists of two main types: Permanent Magnet and Superconducting MRI systems. Permanent Magnet MRIs utilize fixed magnets to generate a magnetic field, offering a compact and cost-effective solution, though usually with lower field strength. In contrast, Superconducting MRI systems employ superconducting magnets to achieve higher magnetic fields, enabling superior imaging quality and faster scan times, but at a higher cost and complexity. Both types cater to diverse clinical needs and healthcare settings.</p></p>
