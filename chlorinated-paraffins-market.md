<p><p>Chlorinated paraffins are categorized based on their chlorination levels and chain lengths, with types such as CP-42, CP-52, and CP-70 representing different carbon chain lengths and chlorine contents. CP-42 typically has a medium level of chlorination, making it suitable for various applications like metalworking fluids. CP-52 offers higher chlorination, which enhances flame retardancy in plastics, while CP-70, being more highly chlorinated, is often used in specialized industrial applications. Additional types may include variants tailored for specific needs, contributing to a diverse market.</p></p>
