<p><ul><li>High-efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA)</li><li>Electrostatic Precipitators (ESPs)</li><li>Ionizers and Ozone Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential air purification equipment market includes various technologies designed to improve indoor air quality. High-efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) filters capture small particles, including dust and allergens. Electrostatic Precipitators (ESPs) use electric charges to trap contaminants, while Ionizers release charged ions to attract and settle airborne pollutants. Ozone Generators produce ozone to eliminate odors and pathogens; however, they can produce harmful byproducts. Each type offers distinct benefits and limitations tailored to different air purification needs.</p></p>
