<p><p>The Dynamic Stability Control (DSC) sensors market can be categorized into two primary types: those with Traction Control System (TCS) and those without TCS. Sensors equipped with TCS enhance vehicle stability by preventing wheel spin during acceleration, improving handling on slippery surfaces. In contrast, sensors without TCS focus solely on maintaining vehicle stability during cornering and dynamic driving conditions. Both types are crucial for optimizing safety and performance in modern vehicles, contributing to overall driving comfort and control.</p></p>
