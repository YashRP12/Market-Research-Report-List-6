<p><p>Cartridge heaters are essential components in industrial heating applications, available in various types. Standard cartridge heaters are versatile, featuring a metal sheath that contains resistive wire for even heating, commonly used in molds and dies. Swaged cartridge heaters, on the other hand, are designed with a tighter fit, enhancing thermal conductivity and efficiency. Their compact design makes them ideal for applications requiring precise temperature control. Both types cater to different heating needs across various industries, including plastics, food processing, and manufacturing.</p></p>
