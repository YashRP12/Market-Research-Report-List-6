<p><p>Probe cards are essential tools used in semiconductor testing. Cantilever probe cards have flexible, spring-like probes that can bend to make contact with the testing points. Vertical probe cards use vertical probes that make direct contact with the testing points. MEMS probe cards integrate microelectromechanical systems for precise testing. Each type of probe card has its own advantages depending on the specific testing requirements. The market for these probe cards continues to grow as the demand for advanced semiconductor testing increases.</p></p>
