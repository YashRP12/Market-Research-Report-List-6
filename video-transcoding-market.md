<p><p>The video transcoding market consists of various codecs such as H.264, H.265, VP9, and AV1. H.264 is widely adopted for its efficiency and quality, leading in legacy applications. H.265 offers better compression and quality for 4K content, but with higher computational demands. VP9, developed by Google, provides similar benefits to H.265 with open-source advantages, often used in web streaming. AV1 aims to surpass both with superior compression rates for streaming, enabling high-quality content delivery with reduced bandwidth costs.</p></p>
