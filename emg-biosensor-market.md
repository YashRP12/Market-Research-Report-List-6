<p><ul><li>Surface Biosensors</li><li>Intramuscular Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EMG biosensors are primarily categorized into two types: surface biosensors and intramuscular biosensors. Surface biosensors are placed on the skin's surface to measure muscle activity non-invasively, making them suitable for various applications such as sports training and rehabilitation. In contrast, intramuscular biosensors are implanted within muscles to provide more accurate and detailed data, making them ideal for research and clinical settings. Both types of biosensors play a crucial role in monitoring and analyzing muscle activity for various purposes.</p></p>
