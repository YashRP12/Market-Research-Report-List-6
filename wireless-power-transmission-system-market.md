<p><p>The Wireless Power Transmission System market comprises several types, including inductive coupling, resonant inductive coupling, and capacitive coupling. Inductive coupling uses electromagnetic fields to transfer energy between coils, making it ideal for short-distance applications like charging pads. Resonant inductive coupling enhances efficiency over larger distances through tuned resonant circuits. Capacitive coupling employs electric fields to transfer power, suitable for specific applications like medical devices. Other methods may include laser-based or microwave transmission, catering to diverse energy needs in various industries.</p></p>
