<p><p>The gastrointestinal videoscope market comprises two primary types: hard mirror and flexible soft mirror. Hard mirror videoscopes feature rigid structures, allowing for direct visualization of the gastrointestinal tract, often used in surgical procedures or diagnostics requiring precise angles. In contrast, flexible soft mirror videoscopes utilize a malleable design, enabling access to curved or difficult-to-reach areas with ease and minimal discomfort to patients. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing gastroenterological diagnostics and treatment, offering distinct advantages based on procedural needs.</p></p>
