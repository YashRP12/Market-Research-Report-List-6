<p><p>The SMD DC-DC converter market is primarily categorized into two types: isolated and non-isolated converters. Isolated converters provide electrical separation between input and output, enhancing safety and noise immunity, making them suitable for applications requiring ground potential differences. Non-isolated converters, on the other hand, do not provide such separation, offering simpler designs with higher efficiency for applications where electrical isolation is not critical. Both types cater to diverse applications in consumer electronics, industrial systems, and renewable energy.</p></p>
