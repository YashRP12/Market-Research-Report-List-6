<p><p>The Styrene Butadiene (SB) binders market is primarily divided into two types: emulsion type and solution type. Emulsion-type SB binders are water-based and known for their ease of application, making them popular in adhesives, coatings, and sealants. In contrast, solution-type SB binders are solvent-based, offering higher performance in certain applications, such as tires and industrial products. Both types are vital in industries like construction, automotive, and textiles, contributing to the overall versatility and performance of final products.</p></p>
