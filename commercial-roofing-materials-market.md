<p><p>The commercial roofing materials market comprises various types, each with unique characteristics. Spray polyurethane foam provides insulation and waterproofing through a seamless application. Built-up roofing features multiple layers of asphalt and felt for robust protection. Lastly, metal roofing includes steel and aluminum, known for longevity and energy efficiency, making it a popular choice.</p></p>
