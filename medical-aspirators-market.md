<p><p>Medical aspirators are devices used to remove bodily fluids, such as mucus and blood, from the airways of patients who have difficulty breathing. There are three main types of medical aspirators: electric, manual, and pneumatic. Electric aspirators are powered by electricity and provide consistent suction power. Manual aspirators are operated by hand and are typically used in emergency situations or in areas without electricity. Pneumatic aspirators are powered by compressed air and are often used in hospital settings for suctioning during surgeries or other medical procedures.</p></p>
