<p><p>Ultra-high-speed machine vision cameras are a crucial component in various industries for real-time imaging and inspection applications. There are two main types of machine vision cameras in the market: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images line by line, suited for continuous objects or fast-moving production lines. Area scan cameras, on the other hand, capture images in a single shot, making them suitable for static objects or smaller areas. Both types play a vital role in ensuring accurate and efficient quality control processes.</p></p>
