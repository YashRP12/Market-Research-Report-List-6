<p><p>Thrust ball bearings are specialized bearings designed to handle axial loads in rotary applications. The market for these bearings is categorized into single-direction and double-direction bearings. Single-direction thrust ball bearings support axial loads in one direction, making them suitable for applications with unidirectional load conditions. In contrast, double-direction thrust ball bearings can accommodate axial loads from both directions, providing versatility for more complex machinery and systems that require bidirectional support, enhancing their applicability across various industrial sectors.</p></p>
