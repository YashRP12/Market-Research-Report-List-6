<p><p>The carpet market comprises several types, each characterized by distinct weaving techniques. Tufted carpets feature yarn punched through a backing, allowing for quick production. Wilton woven carpets use a jacquard loom to create intricate patterns and durability. Woven Axminster carpets are crafted with multi-colored yarns, offering detailed designs and robust structure. Hand weaving represents artisanal craftsmanship, producing unique, high-quality pieces. Additionally, the "Others" category includes niche styles, such as flatweave and needlefelt, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
