<p><p>The photocurable resin coatings market comprises three key components: monomers, oligomers, and photoinitiators. Monomers are small molecules that polymerize upon exposure to light, forming the foundational structure. Oligomers are medium-sized polymer chains that enhance properties like flexibility and strength. Photoinitiators are compounds that absorb light and trigger the polymerization process, ensuring the resin cures effectively. Together, these elements enable the production of high-performance coatings used in various applications, including automotive, electronics, and furniture.</p></p>
