<p><strong>The Tumor Detection Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ctDNA</li><li>CTC</li><li>Fluid Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tumor Detection Kit market primarily comprises three types: ctDNA (circulating tumor DNA), CTC (circulating tumor cells), and fluid detection. ctDNA focuses on analyzing genetic material released from tumors into the bloodstream, allowing for early detection and monitoring of cancers. CTC targets intact tumor cells circulating in the blood, providing insights into tumor characteristics and metastasis. Fluid detection encompasses tests using various body fluids, such as urine or saliva, to identify tumor markers, supporting non-invasive cancer diagnosis and management.</p></p>
