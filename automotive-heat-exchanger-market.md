<p><ul><li>HVAC Thermal Management</li><li>Powertrain Thermal Management</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive heat exchanger market is primarily divided into two types: HVAC thermal management and powertrain thermal management. HVAC thermal management focuses on regulating the temperature within the passenger cabin, ensuring optimal comfort through systems that transfer heat away from or into the vehicle. In contrast, powertrain thermal management involves controlling the temperature of engine components and related systems, which enhances performance and efficiency while preventing overheating. Both types are essential for improving energy efficiency and reducing emissions in modern vehicles.</p></p>
