<p><p>The Wall-mounted MRI Metal Detector market includes two primary types: Single Pillar Systems and Double Pillar Systems. Single Pillar Systems are designed for compact spaces, featuring a single detection unit that effectively scans individuals for metal objects, making them ideal for smaller facilities.
