<p><strong>The T-Cell Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CAR T Cell Therapy</li><li>TCR Therapy</li><li>TIL Therapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>T-cell therapy encompasses various innovative approaches to treat cancer by harnessing the immune system. CAR T-cell therapy involves engineering a patient's T-cells to express chimeric antigen receptors, enabling them to target and destroy cancer cells. TCR therapy focuses on modifying T-cells to recognize specific tumor-associated antigens, enhancing their ability to target a broader range of cancers. TIL therapy utilizes tumor-infiltrating lymphocytes, expanding these cells in the lab to unleash a potent immune response against tumors. Collectively, they represent a rapidly evolving field in oncology.</p></p>
