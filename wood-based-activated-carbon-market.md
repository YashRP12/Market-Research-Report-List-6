<p><p>The wood-based activated carbon market is categorized into three main types: columnar, granular, and powder. Columnar activated carbon features larger, elongated shapes, ideal for applications requiring efficient gas filtration. Granular activated carbon consists of irregular particles and is widely used for water treatment and air purification due to its high surface area. Powdered activated carbon has a fine particle size, allowing for rapid adsorption in various applications, including pharmaceuticals and food processing, where quick reactions are essential.</p></p>
