<p><p>Biological surgical sealants and adhesives are used in surgical procedures to promote tissue healing and prevent leakage. There are three main types of biological sealants and adhesives based on their composition: fibrin-based, collagen-based, and albumin-based. Fibrin-based sealants mimic the natural blood clotting process, collagen-based sealants provide structural support for tissue regeneration, and albumin-based sealants have adhesive properties that help to seal wounds. Each type of sealant offers unique benefits and applications in the field of surgery.</p></p>
