<p><strong>The AMOLED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AMOLED market features two main types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED displays use an active matrix of thin-film transistors, enabling higher resolutions, faster refresh rates, and better energy efficiency, making them ideal for smartphones and high-end displays. In contrast, PMOLED employs a simpler passive matrix system, suitable for smaller screens and lower resolutions, such as wearables and simple panels. Both technologies have distinct applications, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
