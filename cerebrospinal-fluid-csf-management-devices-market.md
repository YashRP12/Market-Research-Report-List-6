<p><p>Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Management Devices market includes CSF shunts and CSF drainage systems. CSF shunts are devices used to treat conditions like hydrocephalus by diverting excess fluid from the brain to another part of the body. CSF drainage systems, on the other hand, are used to remove excess fluid from the brain through a catheter. Both types of devices play a crucial role in managing CSF levels and treating neurological conditions.</p></p>
