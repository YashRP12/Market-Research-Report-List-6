<p><strong>The Benchtop Fundus Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mydriatic</li><li>Non-mydriatic</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop fundus camera market comprises three main types: mydriatic, non-mydriatic, and hybrid cameras. Mydriatic cameras require pupil dilation for clearer imaging of the retina, making them suitable for comprehensive examinations. Non-mydriatic cameras, on the other hand, allow imaging without pupil dilation, facilitating quicker assessments often used in screening settings. Hybrid cameras combine features from both types, offering flexibility for varying clinical needs, enabling detailed retinal imaging while accommodating different patient scenarios.</p></p>
