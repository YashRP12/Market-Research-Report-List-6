<p><p>Induction relays are essential components in electrical systems, categorized into various types based on their operational principles. Electromagnetic relays utilize magnetic fields for switching, while solid state relays employ semiconductor devices for faster response and durability. Time relays control operations based on time delays, and temperature relays activate based on thermal changes. Photorelays respond to light levels, acoustic relays to sound signals, and thermal relays to heat conditions. Each type serves specific applications in automation and protection systems.</p></p>
