<p><strong>The Handheld Medical Infrared Thermometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forehead Type Thermometer</li><li>Ear Type Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld medical infrared thermometer market is primarily categorized into two types: forehead and ear thermometers. Forehead type thermometers utilize infrared technology to measure temperature from the forehead, providing quick and non-invasive readings, making them popular for all age groups. Ear type thermometers, also known as tympanic thermometers, measure temperature within the ear canal, offering accurate results by detecting infrared heat from the eardrum. Both types are efficient for fever detection and are increasingly favored in clinical and home settings.</p></p>
