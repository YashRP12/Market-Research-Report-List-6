<p><strong>The Optical Branching Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FBC</li><li>PLC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical branching device market includes two primary types: Fused Biconical Taper (FBC) and Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) devices. FBC devices utilize the fusion of optical fibers to create a taper, allowing for efficient light splitting and combining, primarily used in fiber optic networks. Meanwhile, PLC devices employ planar processing to integrate multiple optical functions on a single chip, providing greater flexibility and scalability. Both types serve crucial roles in telecommunications, enhancing signal distribution and network efficiency.</p></p>
