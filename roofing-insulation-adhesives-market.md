<p><p>The roofing insulation adhesives market is categorized into three main types. Paste roofing insulation adhesives are thicker and offer higher bonding strength and stability, making them suitable for vertical installations. Other adhesives include foam-based or solvent-based options, catering to specialized applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on ease of application, bonding capability, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
