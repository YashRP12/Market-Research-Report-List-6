<p><p>Flexible PCB market can be classified into Single Layer, Double Layer, and Multi-Layer types. Single layer Flexible PCBs consist of a single layer of base material with a conductive layer on top. Double layer Flexible PCBs have two layers of base material with a conductive layer on each side. Multi-layer Flexible PCBs have three or more layers of base material with multiple conductive layers in between. Each type of Flexible PCB offers different levels of complexity and functionality to cater to diverse electronic applications.</p></p>
