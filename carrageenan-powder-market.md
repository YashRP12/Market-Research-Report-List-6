<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Carrageenan Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kappa Carrageenan</li><li>Iota Carrageenan</li><li>Lambda Carrageenan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carrageenan powder market is segmented into three main types: Kappa, Iota, and Lambda carrageenan. Kappa carrageenan forms rigid gels in the presence of potassium ions, making it suitable for dairy and meat applications. Iota carrageenan produces softer gels in the presence of calcium ions, often used in desserts and jellies. Lambda carrageenan, which does not gel, is utilized as a thickening agent in sauces and dairy products. Each type serves distinct purposes in food and industrial applications.</p></p>
