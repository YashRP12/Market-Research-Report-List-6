<p><p>The metal forming machine tools market encompasses three primary types: bulk forming, sheet forming, and powder metal forming. Bulk forming involves processes like forging and extrusion, suitable for producing large components from solid materials. Sheet forming focuses on shaping thin metal sheets through techniques such as stamping and bending, ideal for automotive and appliance parts. Powder metal forming utilizes metal powders to create complex shapes through methods like pressing and sintering, allowing for precision and reduced waste in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
