<p><ul><li>AC/DC</li><li>DC/DC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The switching power supply market is divided mainly into two types: AC/DC and DC/DC converters. AC/DC converters transform alternating current (AC) from the mains into direct current (DC), serving applications like computers and consumer electronics. DC/DC converters, on the other hand, convert one DC voltage level to another, making them ideal for battery-powered devices and renewable energy systems. Both types are essential for efficient power management and are widely used across various industries, enhancing energy efficiency and performance.</p></p>
