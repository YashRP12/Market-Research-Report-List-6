<p><ul><li>Oil-Immersed Amorphous Metal Transformers</li><li>Dry-Type Amorphous Metal Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The amorphous metal transformers market is divided into two primary types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed amorphous metal transformers use oil as a cooling medium, enhancing thermal management and efficiency, making them suitable for high-capacity applications. In contrast, dry-type amorphous metal transformers do not use oil, relying on air for cooling, which reduces fire risk and maintenance concerns, making them ideal for urban areas and environments with strict safety regulations. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
