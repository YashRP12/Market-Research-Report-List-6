<p><p>Non PVC IV solution bags can be classified into two main types - single-chambered and multi-chambered. Single-chambered bags contain only one chamber for holding the IV solution, making them suitable for simple infusion processes. On the other hand, multi-chambered bags have multiple compartments that allow for the mixing of different medications or fluids before administration. This makes them useful for more complex treatments that require the delivery of multiple substances simultaneously. Both types cater to different needs and preferences within the healthcare industry.</p></p>
