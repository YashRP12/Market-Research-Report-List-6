<p><p>The person-to-person payment market is divided into two main types: remote payments and proximity payments. Remote payments involve transactions conducted over the internet, utilizing apps or online platforms to transfer funds without the need for face-to-face interaction. In contrast, proximity payments occur when users exchange money in close physical range, often using technologies like QR codes or NFC (Near Field Communication). Both types enable users to send and receive money conveniently, catering to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
