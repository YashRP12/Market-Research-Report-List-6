<p><strong>The Cross-Linked Polyethylene Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peroxide Cross-Linked Pipe</li><li>Silane Cross-Linked Pipe</li><li>Irradiation Cross-Linked Pipe</li><li>Azo Cross-Linked Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cross-linked polyethylene (PEX) pipe market comprises several types based on their cross-linking methods. Peroxide cross-linked pipes use peroxide for enhanced thermal and chemical resistance. Silane cross-linked pipes involve a silane reaction that improves flexibility and joint reliability. Irradiation cross-linked pipes utilize electron beam or gamma radiation for stronger molecular structures, ideal for high-temperature applications. Azo cross-linked pipes utilize azo compounds for cross-linking, offering improved mechanical properties and resistance to extreme conditions. Each type serves specific application needs in plumbing and heating systems.</p></p>
