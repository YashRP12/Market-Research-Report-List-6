<p><strong>The Aqueous Polyurethane Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion</li><li>Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aqueous polyurethane resins market is primarily divided into two types: aqueous polyurethane dispersions (PUDs) and aqueous polyurethane emulsions. Aqueous polyurethane dispersions are characterized by their high-performance properties, offering excellent adhesion, flexibility, and resistance to chemicals and weathering. They are commonly used in coatings and adhesives. Aqueous polyurethane emulsions, on the other hand, are typically utilized in textile and leather applications, providing a softer finish and enhancing breathability. Both types are environmentally friendly due to their low solvent content.</p></p>
