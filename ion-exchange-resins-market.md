<p><strong>The Ion Exchange Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anion Exchange Resin</li><li>Cation Exchange Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ion exchange resins market is primarily segmented into anion exchange resins and cation exchange resins. Anion exchange resins target negatively charged ions, effectively removing contaminants like nitrates and phosphates from water, making them essential in water treatment and industrial applications. Conversely, cation exchange resins focus on positively charged ions, facilitating applications such as softening hard water and purifying acids. Both types are crucial for enhancing water quality and enabling various chemical processes across multiple industries.</p></p>
