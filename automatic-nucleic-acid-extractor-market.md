<p><p>The automatic nucleic acid extractor market features primarily two types: the impertic bead type and straw type. The impertic bead type utilizes magnetic or silica beads to bind and purify nucleic acids, providing high efficiency and reproducibility. In contrast, the straw type employs a hollow straw-like design to extract nucleic acids, allowing for a more straightforward and user-friendly process. Both types cater to various applications in molecular biology, diagnostics, and research, enhancing the speed and accuracy of nucleic acid extraction.</p></p>
