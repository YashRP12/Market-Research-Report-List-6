<p><p>Atrial Fibrillation Treatment Device market offers different types of treatment options such as Catheter Ablation and Maze Surgery. Catheter Ablation is a minimally invasive procedure where a catheter is used to destroy small areas of heart tissue that are causing abnormal heart rhythms. Maze Surgery involves creating a pattern of scar tissue in the heart to block abnormal electrical signals. Both procedures aim to restore a normal heart rhythm and improve symptoms of atrial fibrillation.</p></p>
