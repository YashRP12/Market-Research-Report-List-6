<p><p>Ambulatory Electronic Health Record (EHR) and Electronic Medical Record (EMR) systems are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based systems are hosted online, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from multiple devices, while reducing the need for extensive IT infrastructure. On-premise systems, however, are installed locally within healthcare facilities, providing greater control over data security and integration with existing systems, but often requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Each option caters to different operational needs and budgets.</p></p>
