<p><p>Ultrasonic proximity sensors are categorized into two market types - Analog Type and Digital Type. Analog Type sensors provide a continuous output signal that varies with the distance of the object from the sensor. On the other hand, Digital Type sensors provide discrete output signals, typically in the form of a binary signal indicating the presence or absence of an object within a certain range. Both market types cater to different applications and industries requiring proximity sensing technology.</p></p>
