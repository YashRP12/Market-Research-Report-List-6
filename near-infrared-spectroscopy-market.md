<p><p>Near-infrared spectroscopy market types include FT-NIR (Fourier-Transform Near-Infrared) and UV-Vis-NIR (Ultraviolet-Visible-Near-Infrared). FT-NIR technology offers high resolution and accuracy, making it ideal for research and quality control applications. UV-Vis-NIR spectroscopy combines ultraviolet, visible, and near-infrared light to provide a broader range of analysis capabilities. Both types of spectroscopy are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, agriculture, and environmental monitoring for chemical analysis, material identification, and process control.</p></p>
