<p><p>An Avalanche Photodiode (APD) detector is a semiconductor device that converts light into an electrical current. It operates by applying a high reverse-bias voltage, which amplifies the photocurrent generated when photons are absorbed. This amplification is due to the avalanche effect, where a single incident photon can generate multiple charge carriers, making APDs highly sensitive and suitable for low-light applications.</p><p>The Avalanche Photodiode Detector Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand in telecommunications, medical imaging, and consumer electronics.
