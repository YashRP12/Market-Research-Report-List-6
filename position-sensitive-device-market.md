<p><ul><li>One Dimensional</li><li>Two Dimensional</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Position Sensitive Device (PSD) market consists of two main types: one-dimensional and two-dimensional devices. One-dimensional PSDs measure position along a single axis, typically providing data on linear movement or distance. In contrast, two-dimensional PSDs capture positional information in a plane, allowing for more complex tracking and mapping of movement in both horizontal and vertical directions. Each type serves specific applications, with one-dimensional devices often used in simpler setups and two-dimensional devices suited for advanced navigation and imaging tasks.</p></p>
