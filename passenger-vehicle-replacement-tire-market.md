<p><p>The passenger vehicle replacement tire market is primarily categorized into radial and bias tires. Radial tires feature a construction where the fibers run perpendicular to the tire's direction of travel, offering improved fuel efficiency, better traction, and enhanced safety. They are commonly used for modern vehicles. In contrast, bias tires have plies that run diagonally, providing more stability and durability, making them suitable for off-road or rugged conditions. Each type caters to different driving needs and preferences, influencing consumer choices.</p></p>
