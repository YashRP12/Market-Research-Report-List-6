<p><strong>The FRAM Storage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Serial FRAM</li><li>Parallel FRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FRAM storage is categorized into Serial FRAM and Parallel FRAM, each serving distinct application needs. Serial FRAM utilizes a single data line for communication, making it suitable for low-pin-count environments and simpler designs. Meanwhile, Parallel FRAM features multiple data lines, allowing for faster data transfer rates and higher throughput, ideal for applications requiring rapid access to large amounts of data. Both types are valued for their non-volatility, low power consumption, and endurance, catering to diverse markets such as consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications.</p></p>
