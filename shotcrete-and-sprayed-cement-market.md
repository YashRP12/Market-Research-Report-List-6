<p><p>Shotcrete, or sprayed cement, encompasses two main application methods: wet spraying and dry spraying. Wet spraying involves pre-mixing cement, water, and additives before delivering the mixture through a hose to the surface, allowing for a cohesive application ideal for complex shapes. In contrast, dry spraying delivers dry cement and aggregates through a hose, where water is added at the nozzle. This technique is often quicker and offers better precision in applications, catering to various construction and repair needs.</p></p>
