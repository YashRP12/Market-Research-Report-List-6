<p><p>The car carrier market comprises two primary types: open-air car carriers and enclosed car carriers. Open-air carriers transport vehicles on multi-level trailers without protective coverings, making them a cost-effective option for standard vehicles. Conversely, enclosed car carriers provide protection from environmental elements, offering secure transport for luxury or classic cars. These carriers are often equipped with additional features to safeguard the vehicles during transit, catering to clients who prioritize security and condition over cost.</p></p>
