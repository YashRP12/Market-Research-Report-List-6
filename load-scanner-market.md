<p><p>The load scanner market is divided into visible light and invisible light types, each serving unique applications in load measurement and monitoring. Visible light load scanners utilize cameras and sensors that capture images of cargo, providing detailed visual data for analysis. In contrast, invisible light load scanners employ technologies like infrared or laser scanning to gather information without direct visibility. Both types cater to industries such as logistics, transportation, and warehousing, optimizing efficiency and accuracy in load management processes.</p></p>
