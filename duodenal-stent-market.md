<p><p>The duodenal stent market comprises two primary types: polymer duodenal stents and metal duodenal stents. Polymer duodenal stents are lightweight and flexible, designed for temporary conditions, offering ease of deployment and reduced risk of tissue hyperplasia. Metal duodenal stents, typically made from materials like steel or alloy, provide greater strength and durability, making them suitable for long-term use in obstructive cases. Both types are essential for treating duodenal obstructions, enhancing patient outcomes in gastrointestinal care.</p></p>
